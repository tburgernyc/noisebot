# Edge Research — July 2026 (primary-source survey for next registrations)

Method: 5 parallel research passes (overnight premium, vol/carry, event &
calendar flows, crypto, meta/decay evidence), primary sources preferred
(peer-reviewed, Fed/NBER/BIS/SEC, exchange disclosures). Full citations at
bottom of each section summary. Purpose: rank REGISTERABLE hypotheses.
Nothing here is forward expectancy.

## Base rates (meta-evidence — read first)

- Post-publication anomaly decay ~26% OOS, ~58% post-publication (McLean &
  Pontiff JF 2016); newer signals decay FASTER every publication year
  (Falck/Rej/Thesmar QF 2022). Categories that survive: slow risk premia,
  low-capacity/illiquid effects. Dies first: high-turnover microstructure —
  exactly intraday index futures.
- Brazil full-population study: 97% of persistent futures day traders lose;
  1.1% earn > minimum wage (Chague et al. 2020). Taiwan: <1% predictably
  profitable (Barber/Odean lineage).
- Prop-firm primary disclosure (Topstep 2025): 33.3% of funded participants
  ever get ANY payout; 0.71% reach live capital. Assume ≤5-10% probability
  of net-positive income after all fees via evals.
- Realistic prior for a registered hypothesis passing our gates: 10-20%.

## Falsified / non-viable at $2-5k (do not register variants)

- OVERNIGHT DRIFT (ES/NQ): dead. NY Fed's own July 2026 update: flat since
  2021 (imbalance mechanism's fuel halved); NightShares ETFs = real-money
  falsification (-6.9% vs +22% SPX, closed 2023).
- SHORT VOL / VRP: premium real (PUT index +9-18%/yr 2023-25) but one short
  VXM into a 2018/Aug-2024 repeat = -$1,600..-$4,000 overnight; incompatible
  with $2-5k AND with trailing-drawdown props (CFE products not even
  permitted). Revisit at $10-25k, defined-risk only.
- CARRY/TERM STRUCTURE: needs 15-25 market breadth; micro suite + $2-5k
  can't provide it. Treasury basis: financing-gated to hedge funds.
- PEAD: gone from tradeable stocks since ~2006 (Martineau CFR); survives
  only in microcaps where spreads eat it.
- OPEX WEEK, DAY-OF-WEEK, classic US TURN-OF-MONTH: decayed/dead in recent
  data. Textbook mined calendar effects.
- FLASH-LOAN / DEX ARB: solo dev is the liquidity, not the arbitrageur
  (~70% of public bots don't run, ~4% work and get front-run; Flashbots).
- CME micro BTC basis: compressed to ~5% ann.; sub-T-bill economics at size.

## RANKED registerable candidates

### 1. Last-hour flow momentum (MNQ) — best testability
Mechanism: market-maker/leveraged-ETF gamma hedging near the close makes
rest-of-day return predict the last 30-60 min (Baltussen/Da/Lammers/Martens
JFE 2021: 60+ futures, Sharpe 0.87-1.73 at asset-class level; Dim/Eraker/
Vilkov 2024: dealer-gamma sign conditions it in the 0DTE era).
Fit: ~250 trades/yr → validatable on our EXISTING 2-yr Databento window.
Per-trade edge 2-5 bps ($10-25/MNQ) vs ~$3.50 costs — execution-sensitive.
Honesty flag: closest to the falsified momentum family; distinct mechanism
(close-anchored hedging flow, specific window) but treat proximity as a
prior AGAINST, and pre-register strictly.

### 2. Month-end rebalancing pressure (MES/MNQ) — best mechanism
Mechanism: mandated pension/target-weight rebalancing; when equities are
overweight vs bonds late in the month, next-day equity returns lower by
~17 bps, strongest last 4 trading days, reverts within ~2 weeks (Harvey/
Mazzoleni/Melone NBER WP 33554, 2025 — ES + 10Y futures 1997-2023;
~$16B/yr transfer). Clears MNQ costs ~20x.
Fit problem: 12-40 signals/yr → n≥100 needs ~8+ years of data (Databento
ES/MNQ history purchase, cheap) and gates need adapting for multi-day
holds (prop rules: no overnight → this is an IBKR/live-account edge, or
day-session-only expression must be pre-registered). Freshly published →
expect partial decay; zero post-2023 OOS yet.

### 3. Slow BTC/ETH long-only trend (spot) — best net-of-cost evidence
Mechanism: slow-moving capital + retail underreaction; value = downside
avoidance, not excess return (Han/Kang/Ryu: net Sharpe 1.51 vs 0.85
buy-hold at 15 bps costs, 2014-2023; Kang/Ryu Risk Mgmt 2026: slow ~12-wk
signals beat fast). Decay documented post-ETF (Rosen/Wang 2025).
Fit: trades few times/month on Coinbase/Kraken spot — fee-tolerant, no
PDT, automatable. n≥100 requires long daily-data backtest (free data) +
patience live. NOT income: it's compounding with smaller drawdowns.
Funding-rate overlay on new CFTC-regulated US perps (Coinbase 7/2025,
Kraken 6/2026): single-digit APR = $100-400/yr at our size. Real, small.

## Income reality (the sentence that matters)

At $2-5k, even a TRUE passing edge yields hundreds of dollars per year,
not living income. Every credible source converges: the solo-quant path is
compounding + skill; income comes from selling the skill (freelance bot
builds) while the pipeline runs. Anyone promising more is contradicted by
the base rates above.
